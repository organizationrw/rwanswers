Open-ended Questions

NOTE! Please make sure to change the answer a bit. DO NOT COPY AND PASTE!


What “first” did the comic book character Black Panther achieve with its debut in 1966?

Black Panther was the first Black superhero in American comics.


According to Brookings.com, what did Black Panther's success prove about diverse and inclusive movies?

Brookings.com reported that Black Panther's success proved the economic power of diversity and inclusion.


Why was the movie Black Panther so popular? Give at least two reasons from the text to support your answer.

Answers may vary but should be supported by the text. Students could discuss the excellent acting or the inventive world of Wakanda. They could also discuss the satisfaction audiences got from seeing a world of Black heroes and characters in a major Hollywood production. They could also talk about the conditions of its release: it was heavily publicized and released to a huge, international audience.
